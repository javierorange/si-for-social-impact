Active Pathways

Your first steps to become more active

How might we

How might we help older adults live healthier and happier lives by increasing their physical activity?

Active Pathways is a web application to enable older adults to take their first steps towards a more active lifestyle.Some older adults had a problem at staying fit and wanted a simpler and more accessible way.
We designed a coaching small bite programme with actionable daily challenges with reminders. So far, 2000 participants engaged with our programme. Active Pathways enable older adults to feel more confident, increase their wellbeing and change gradually their life.
